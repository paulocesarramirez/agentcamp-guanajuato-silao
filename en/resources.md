## List of Resources to Learn About AI Agents

Based on the context of AgentCamp Guanajuato-Silao, I have integrated the best resources available in Spanish (prioritizing official versions from Microsoft Learn, Azure, and related repositories). The list is organized by the event paths: **AI Accelerators** (focused on business leaders, strategies, and agent solutions for decision-making) and **AI Creators** (focused on engineers and developers, with emphasis on creation, prototyping, and best practices). Each path includes relevant categories, with a maximum of 10 resources in total.

### Path: AI Accelerators
This path focuses on accelerating AI adoption in business environments, with lessons for SMEs, strategies, and tools for agent solutions.

#### Fundamentals and Strategies (5 resources)
1. Podcast "Best of the Month for AI Accelerators": YouTube playlist with monthly summaries and key highlights for business leaders in AI agent adoption and strategies. [https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLBld54uQEsi6KMiIXD8WqvAKSq0RFf7vA](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLBld54uQEsi6KMiIXD8WqvAKSq0RFf7vA)
2. AI Learning Center: Journey for business leaders in responsible AI adoption, including planning and scaling agents. [https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/ai/](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/ai/)
3. Creating an Artificial Intelligence Strategy: Guide to building effective AI strategies using Microsoft technologies, with a focus on adoption. [https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/cloud-adoption-framework/scenarios/ai/strategy](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/cloud-adoption-framework/scenarios/ai/strategy)
4. AI Learning Resources: AI highlights, fundamentals, and community connections to scale agent projects. [https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/ai/ai-resources/](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/ai/ai-resources/)
5. Introduction to AI Agent Development in Azure: Basic concepts about AI agents and their use in Microsoft Azure for decision-making. [https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/training/modules/ai-agent-fundamentals/](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/training/modules/ai-agent-fundamentals/)

#### Platforms and Tools (3 resources)
6. Azure AI Foundry: Comprehensive platform to explore, build, operate, and monitor AI solutions, including agents. [https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/products/ai-foundry/](https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/products/ai-foundry/)
7. Azure AI Foundry Agent Service: Tools to create and deploy AI agents with built-in security. [https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/products/ai-agent-service/](https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/products/ai-agent-service/)
8. Foundry Tools: AI tool portfolio to create agents quickly, with business cases. [https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/products/ai-foundry/tools](https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/products/ai-foundry/tools)

#### Demonstrations and Cases (2 resources)
9. Microsoft AI Tour Resources (2025-2026): Resources to create safe and intelligent agents with Azure AI Foundry and protocols like MCP. [https://adoption.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-ai-tour-resources/](https://adoption.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-ai-tour-resources/)
10. Azure AI Platform: Solutions for conversational AI and agents in business environments. [https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/solutions/ai](https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/solutions/ai)
